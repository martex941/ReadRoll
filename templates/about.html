About ReadRoll

ReadRoll is my CS50x final project. The website's purpose is to generate a random book based on the user's input. The core idea is to help people who have a difficult time choosing new books to read. With the help of ReadRoll the user's worries are taken away as the choice is randomized.

The website is based on Google Books API. Apart from its main feature it allows the user to look into their history of rolled books, as well as, add a rolled book to their library once the user is logged in. Every time you roll a book there are also quick links to buy it in various formats.
